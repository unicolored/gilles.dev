My career so far

From 2021 to 2024, I led the charge on 2 amazing projects for Stellantis' customers: an AI-driven vehicle Search Engine and an Order Tracking module for e-commerce platforms. I worked with teams from around the globe, connecting me with diverse cultures and perspectives.

In 2015, I joined Groupe PSA’s Digital Factory and became a key player in facilitating Innovation committees and developing proof of concepts on the latest trends: Internet-Of-Things, Real-time 3D, Progressive Web Apps, Chatbots & Vocal Assistants.

The beginning

In 2006, I worked in an advertising agency that quickly became a powerhouse, thanks to a great creative director and self-taught skills in producing media.

My first big win? Writing Scan & Retouche d'Images, a book on photo manipulation for MicroApplication that sold ~20,000 copies.

Before that, I studied academic drawings, video montage and art history (but that's old news now).
